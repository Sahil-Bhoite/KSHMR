The Process

Mastery in Every Detail

The Art of Pashmina

Harvested from the Changthangi goat at high altitudes, the wool is hand-spun and hand-woven. A single shawl can take months to complete.

Walnut Wood Carving

Indigenous to Kashmir, Walnut wood is known for its durability. Master carvers use intricate chiseling techniques to create deep relief designs.

Paper Mache

Sakhtsazi (making the object) and Naqashi (painting) come together to create vibrant, intricate decor pieces using gold leaf and mineral colors.
