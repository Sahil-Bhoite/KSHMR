The Art of Pashmina

Harvested from the Changthangi goat at high altitudes, the wool is hand-spun and hand-woven. A single shawl can take months to complete, embodying the pinnacle of softness and warmth.

Walnut Wood Carving

Indigenous to Kashmir, Walnut wood is known for its durability and rich color. Master carvers use intricate chiseling techniques to create deep, relief, and undercut designs inspired by flora and fauna.

Paper Mache

Sakhtsazi (making the object) and Naqashi (painting the surface) come together to create vibrant, intricate decor pieces. Gold leaf and mineral colors bring these masterpieces to life.
